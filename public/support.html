Billing & subscriptions

If you subscribed on iOS, you can manage your subscription in Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. For web billing questions, email support@mymeridianapp.com.

Common questions

How do I cancel my subscription?
On iOS: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Meridian > Cancel. On web: go to your Profile in the app and tap "Manage subscription." Cancellation takes effect at the end of your billing period.

How do I delete my account?
Email privacy@mymeridianapp.com and we'll remove your account and all associated data within 48 hours.

Is my data private?
Yes. We never sell, share, or use your data to train AI models. Read our full Privacy Policy.
