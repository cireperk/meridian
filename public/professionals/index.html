What your clients use it for

Privacy your clients can trust

This matters. Your clients are sharing sensitive things. Here's how we handle it:

  • Encrypted. Conversations and documents are encrypted and never accessible to anyone — including us.
  • No shared access. There is no co-parent portal. Nothing a client writes is visible to their ex, a court, or any third party.
  • Not legal advice. Meridian is clear with users that it doesn't replace professional counsel. It complements your work — it doesn't compete with it.
  • Full deletion. Clients can request complete data deletion at any time. No lock-in.
