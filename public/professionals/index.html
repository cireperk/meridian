Give your clients support between sessions

Meridian is an AI-powered companion for people navigating divorce and co-parenting. When your clients leave your office, Meridian is there at midnight, on the weekend, and in the parking lot before a handoff — helping them stay grounded, communicate clearly, and understand their documents.

What your clients get

📄

Decree analysis in plain English

Clients upload their custody decree or divorce agreement and get a clear, jargon-free breakdown of their rights, obligations, and key dates. Fewer "what does this mean?" calls to your office.

💬

Communication coaching

Before sending a heated text to their co-parent, clients can draft it in Meridian first. The AI coach helps them rewrite messages that are clear, calm, and court-appropriate — reducing conflict and protecting their case.

📅

Custody calendar & schedule tracking

Clients set up their custody pattern (2-2-3, week-on/week-off, etc.) and see at a glance whose days are whose, upcoming handoffs, and important deadlines — all in one place.

💚

24/7 emotional support

A grounding AI companion that listens without judgment, helps clients process difficult moments, and supports healthier decision-making — available anytime, not just during office hours.
